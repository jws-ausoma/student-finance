How to use this dashboard

AUSOMA students are grouped by program phase. Each phase has its own fee model and reconciliation page:

  • Basic Sciences — PreMed (PM1-PM4) and MD1-MD5 students. Per-semester tuition; check per-term breakdown to confirm each semester is invoiced.
  • Pre-Clinical — students charged monthly LOA / ASF fees during the gap between Basic Sciences and Clinical Rotations.
  • Clinical Sciences — students with active rotations. Per-rotation billing.
  • AR Aging — outstanding QBO balances bucketed by days overdue. Cross-phase view.
  • Exceptions Queue — all open flags sorted by severity and amount. Start here daily.
